Focus - Brazil's Market Readout

The Focus Market Readout is a weekly publication by the Central Bank of Brazil that consolidates forecasts from economists and financial institutions regarding key economic indicators such as inflation (IPCA), GDP growth, interest rates (Selic), and exchange rates. It serves as a reference for market expectations and helps guide economic analysis and policy decisions.

The report is released every Monday around 8:25 a.m. (Bras√≠lia time), based on estimates submitted during the previous week, up until Friday.
